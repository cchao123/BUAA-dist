Welcome Message

The glymphatic system, first discovered and described in 2012, represents an unique waste clearance pathway in the central nervous system...

Call for papers:

* Anatomy, physiology, and function of the glymphatic system in neurological diseases

* Glymphatic dysfunction in neurodegenerative diseases, stroke, traumatic brain injury, and small-vessel disease

* Imaging, non-invasive assessment, and biomarkers of glymphatic function

* Pharmacologic and non-pharmacologic interventions to modulate glymphatic flow

* Multidisciplinary approaches, artificial intelligence, and computational modeling in the glymphatic system research
